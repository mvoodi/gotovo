Deadlines, required documents, offices, tuition fee, financial aid and scholarships.

Documents Required (IAAU)

  1. Original High School Diploma of Graduation (with transcript).
  2. Original “ORT” (GRT) certificate.
  3. Copy of ID Card.
  4. Copy of Military Registration Certificate (boys only).
  5. ID Photos 3×4 — 8 pieces.
  6. Medical Certificate № 086.
  7. Initial Payment: 30% of total tuition fee.

Financial Aid

Who is eligible?

  • Republican Olympiad winners (Physics, Chemistry, Biology, English, Computer, Kyrgyz, Turkish, Russian): 1st place — 100% (free), 2nd — 50%, 3rd — 40%. Medalists may choose any department.

  • Next-year graduates with 1st place in national Olympiads (same subjects): 25% discount. Medalists may choose any department.

  • International Olympiad (Turkish language): 1st — 100%, 2nd — 50%, 3rd — 40%.

  • GRT (ORT) top results:

    • Top 100 — 100%
    • Top 200 — 50%
    • Top 300 — 40%
    • Top 500 — 30%
    • Top 1000 — 25%
    • Top 2000 — 20%
    • Top 3000 — 15%
    • Top 5000 — 10%

    Each discount below is limited to one quota in our department.

  • “Red” High School Diploma — 15%.
  • 1st place in regional Olympiad — 20%.
  • Students from one family — common 10% discount.

In case of poor progress, a student loses the discount for the next semester. If performance improves in the following semester, the discount will be restored. When transferring to another department, the discount is lost and re-registration occurs under the new department’s contract. In case of withdrawal from the university, the student must pay 20% of the contract.

GRT = ORT (Общий Республиканский Тест)

Limitation: Applicants can benefit from only one of the available discounts.

Scholarships

Starting from the second year, in case of success, three students with the top GPA from each department are awarded:

  • First Place — 2000 soms
  • Second Place — 1500 soms
  • Third Place — 1000 soms

Note

  • Scholarships are granted for one semester only.
  • Besides the scholarship for social activity, a student can take either a discount or another scholarship (e.g., those with 100% or 50% discount cannot take the scholarship).

Important: All of the above information is valid for the 2013–2014 Academic Year and may differ in subsequent years.
