Rules for Examinations

Please read the exam rules carefully.

  1. Only students in the “Examination List” will be allowed to take the examination.
  2. Students must appear at the examination room at least ten minutes before the beginning of the examination.
  3. Candidates shall bring their photo identification (IAAU ID Card, valid Passport, or Driver’s License) and place it on their desks.
  4. Coats, jackets, knapsacks, purses, pencil cases, notes and books must be left in areas designated by the Observer(s). Small items (phone, wallet, laptop, calculator) may be kept under the chair in a bag large enough to contain them. Pencil cases on desks may be searched.
  5. The Observer has authority to assign seats to candidates.
  6. Students shall not communicate with one another during the examination. Leaving the room is not allowed without escort (including washroom).
  7. Bringing unauthorized materials/electronic devices or assisting/receiving assistance is subject to penalties under the Code of Behaviour on Academic Matters, including loss of academic credit and expulsion.
  8. Entering later than 15 minutes after start is generally prohibited; leaving (except under supervision) is not allowed until at least 30 minutes have elapsed.
  9. Candidates shall remain seated during the final ten minutes of the examination.
  10. At the conclusion, all writing must cease immediately. The Chief Presiding Officer may seize papers for non-compliance and a penalty may be imposed.
  11. Examination books and other issued materials shall not be removed from the room except by authority of the Chief Presiding Officer.
The University is not responsible for personal property left in examination rooms.
