Call for Applications for PhD Candidates and Postgraduate Researchers

DAAD Project “Kyrgyz-German Faculty of Applied Informatics (KGFAI)”

About the Project

Within the framework of the DAAD project (2016–2019), a call is announced among lecturers and young researchers of Ala-Too International University and KSUCTA to conduct research at the Kyrgyz-German Faculty of Applied Informatics (KGFAI) and the West Saxon University of Applied Sciences Zwickau (WHZ).

Aim

Goal — to integrate research into teaching and strengthen KGFAI’s academic staff. The key criterion is the relevance of the dissertation topic; the dissertation defense is expected within two years. Research and teaching are carried out at KGFAI; the candidate commits to teach and conduct research at KGFAI for at least three years.

Research Areas

  • Information Security
  • Applied Informatics — Mobile Systems

Academic supervision: KGFAI Dean, Dr. Abdulaev A.A.

Support Provided

  • Honorarium for research at KGFAI — €250/month (50% workload dedicated to research).
  • Research internship up to 3 months at WHZ: travel expenses up to €700 covered, plus a monthly stipend of €1000 (medical insurance in Germany must be purchased from this stipend).

Required Documents (EN/DE)

  1. Completed application form
  2. Detailed dissertation exposé
  3. Recommendation letter from the main supervisor
  4. CV
  5. Language certificate (German or English) at B1 level

Selection Committee

  • KGFAI Dean, Dr. Abdulaev A.A.
  • DAAD Project Manager

Application Deadlines

  • Submission deadline: no later than 31.08.2017
  • Submit to: kgfi.project@gmail.com
  • Results: 15.09.2017

Contacts

Questions: kgfi.project@gmail.com
