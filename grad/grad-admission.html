Admission Requirements

Criteria and required documents for the Master program

Requirements

a) Field of Study Requirements

Bachelor’s or Specialist degree in Informatics with at least 75% academic performance during the last two years of study. Alternatively: a Bachelor’s or Specialist degree in a technical field with at least 85% performance in informatics-related subjects in the last two years. Competencies must be officially documented. Professional experience in programming can be recognized as fulfilling this requirement. If necessary, applicants must take additional advanced programming and software development courses at KGFAI or Ala-Too University (outside evening/weekend time). These courses require additional tuition fees.

b) Foreign Language Requirements

  • English — level B2, or
  • English — level B1 plus German — level B2

English proficiency is assessed by Ala-Too University faculty. German proficiency is assessed by KGFAI KSUCTA faculty.

List of Documents Required

  1. Application form
  2. Original Bachelor’s (or Specialist) diploma with transcript
  3. Copy of diploma supplement
  4. Two photos (3×4 cm)
  5. Passport copy (original required for verification)
  6. Contract
  7. Personal data sheet (CV form)

Note for International Students

Translation of passport, diploma and supplement into Kyrgyz or Russian is required. Translations must be notarized or certified by licensed translation agencies.
