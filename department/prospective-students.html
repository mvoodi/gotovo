Entry Requirements

  • High school diploma (or equivalent).
  • Math readiness (algebra & introductory calculus).
  • English proficiency (interviews/placement may apply).
  • Motivation statement (why CS at AIU).

Specific criteria may vary by program. See the Admission page for the latest details.

Admission Page

What you’ll learn

  • Strong foundations in programming, algorithms, and data structures.
  • Hands-on projects with modern tools and version control.
  • Electives across AI/ML, web, mobile, systems, security.
  • Capstone projects with industry or research labs.
